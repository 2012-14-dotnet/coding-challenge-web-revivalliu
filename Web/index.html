Having completed my studies there, I was duly attached to teh Fifth Northumberaland fusiliers as assistant surgeon. The regiment was stationed at inia at the time, and before i could join it, the second afghan war had broken out. on landing at bombay, i learned that my corps had advanced through the passes, and was already deep in the enemy's country.
